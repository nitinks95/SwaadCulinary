Stuffed Loki Curry

Ingredients for Stuff..

  1. Potatoes : 2(cooked)
  2. Bottle gourd : ½(small)
  3. Chick peas : 184 cup(cooked)
  4. Ginger : 1 tsp(grated)
  5. Garam masala powder : ¼ tsp
  6. Chili powder : ½ tsp
  7. Lemon juice : 1 tsp

Ingredients for gravy..

  1. tomato puree : 1 cup
  2. curds : ½ cup
  3. ginger : ¼ tsp(grated)
  4. poppy seeds : ¼ cup(ground)
  5. garam masala powder : ¼ tsp
  6. chili powder : ¼ tsp

It is very easy to make!..

Peel the bottle gourd and cut into 2 ½ - 3” pieces and remove the pulp and make it hollow. Peel the cooked potato and mash it. Mix it with cooked chick peas, salt, chili powder, garam masala powder & ginger grating. Mix it thoroughly. Fill this in the bottle gourd pieces and steam it. Take the tomato puree in a shallow pan. Add salt, garam masala powder, grated ginger, chili powder, curds and ground poppy seeds and bring it to boil. Now add the steamed bottle gourd stuffings into this and serve hot with chapathi or rice.
