About Author

Veena Pramod
Mrs. Veena Pramod is a highly accomplished person who has varied interest in art, music and She is a graduate in music from Bangalore University. Her perfection and intricate skills have been inherited from her mother Smt. Susheela. She has been a constant source of inspiration and motivation to her daughter. The success of all her endeavors has been possible only because of the constant support and constructive suggestions of her husband, Mr. Pramod Kumar, an officer in ITC.

Cookery and art

Her passion for cooking started very early when she was still a school girl. She is regular cookery columnist for Kannada media, Priyanka magazine and Vijaya Karnataka news-paper. Her cookery and art demos are also very popular in the kannada visual media. She has also worked as a resource person for AWAKE, an organization of women entrepreneurs. From college days she has taken special interest in art. She has learnt Tanjore paintings from Mrs.Sumalatha a renowned artist from Bangalore and Rajasthani paintings from Sri.Uma.Shankar.Sharma a name to reckon. After learning these arts she imparts her knowledge to others. She conducts classes in paintings in Bangalore and Saharanpur as well.
