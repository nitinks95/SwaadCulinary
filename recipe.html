Layered Idly Bars

Ingredients

  1. Foxtail Millet : 1cup
  2. Black gram dal : 1cup
  3. Finger millet semolina : 1cup
  4. Proso millet semolina : 1cup
  5. Chutney powder : 2 tsp
  6. Carrot : 1/2 Cup(grated)
  7. Onion : 1/2 Cup(Chopped)
  8. Green chilly : 1/2 Cup(Crushed)
  9. Groundnuts : 1/4 Cup(Roasted and Crushed)
  10. Chilly powder : 1/2 tsp
  11. Salt : to taste

Recipe

Soak Foxtail Millet semolina, Proso Semolina for an hour in 3/4 cup of hot water each.Soak Blackgram Dhal for one hour and grind it until smooth. Divide the blackgram batter into 3 equal portions & mtx each portion with the soaked foxtail semolina, Finger Millet semolina and proso millet semolina. Allow It to ferment for 5-6 hours. Then add salt & a pinch of soda to the above batter. Grease a square dish and pour foxtail millet batter and stream it for 5 minutes only. Remove from heat and sprinkle chutney powder and pour finger millet batter over this and again steam it for 5 minutes and remove from heat. Now mix grated carrot, onions, green chilli paste, crushed groundnut powder, salt and red chilli powder and spread it on the above half steamed finger millet layer and then cover the same with proso millet batter and steam for 10 to l5 minutes. After steaming cut into squares and apply garlic or tomato chutney on the top and serve hot.

Dhokla

Ingredients

  1. Kodo Millet semolina : 1 cup
  2. Gram flour : 1/3 cup)
  3. Butter milk : 1 cup)
  4. Lemon : 1/2 (optional)
  5. Salt : 1/2 tsp
  6. Fruit salt : 1/2 tsp
  7. For Gamishing*
  8. Carrot : 1/4 cup (grated)
  9. Coconut: 1/4 cup (grated)
  10. Coriander leaves : 1/4 cup (chapped
  11. Mustard seasoning with Red chilli

Recipe

Mix semolina and gram flour. Add buttermilk. If butter milk is not sour add the juice from half a lime. Add salt and fruit salt and mix well. Immediately transfer the batter to a greased vessel and steam it for 15 minutes. After steaming, transfer the dhokla onto a plate and garnish with grated carrot, coconut, coriander leaves and seasoning. Serve either chilled or hot. (If sweetish dhokla is required, after seasoning sprinkle sugar syrup on top or dip the dhokla in sugar water (1 :1 ratio) and then garnish.

Kodo Milet Upma

Ingredients

  1. Little Millet : 1 cup
  2. Greengram dal : 1/2 cup)
  3. Carrot : 1 cup (finely chopped)
  4. Broad beans : 1 cup (chopped)
  5. Green chllli : 3-4 (split lengthwise)
  6. Lemon : 1
  7. Onions : 2 cup (chopped )
  8. Coriander leaves : 1/4 cup (chopped)
  9. Salt : 1/2 tsp
  10. oil : 4-5 tsp
  11. Mustard : 1/2 tsp
  12. Cumin : 1 tsp
  13. Black gram dal : 1 tsp
  14. Bengal gram dal : 2 tsp
  15. curry leaves : few
  16. coconut : 1/2 cup (grated ) (optional)

Recipe

Cook millet With 2 cups of water. Boil and strain green gram dal. Parboil broad beans and carrot. Take oil in a vessel and heat it. Add mustard and cumin seeds. When it sputters, add black gram dal, bengal gram dal, green chillies and curry leaves. Fry well. Add chopped onion and fry till translucent. Add parboiled vegetables, salt, lemon ju1ce and boiled green gram and millet. Mix well. Garnish with coriander leaves. Grated coconut can be added if required.

Spicy Jower Balls

Ingredients

  1. Jowar/sorghum flour : 1 cup
  2. water : 1(1/4) cup
  3. onions : 1 (chopped)
  4. carrots : 2 grated
  5. Methi leaves : 1 cup(chopped)
  6. salt : 1/2 tsp
  7. Lemon : 1/2
  8. Mustard : 1/2 tsp
  9. Cumin seeds : 1 tsp
  10. Oil : 1 tsp
  11. Coconut : 1 tsp(grated)

Recipe

Boil 1 1/4 cup of water in a vessel. Add a pinch of salt to it, when the water comes to a boil, add the sorghum flour and close the lid for 5 minutes. Remove from heat and mix well such that no lumps are formed. Knead the dough and make small marble sized balls from the above dough and steam them for 10-15 minutes. Heat Oil in a pan. add mustard, cumin, onions and fry well. Now add methi leaves, carrots to this and mix. Fmally add steamed jowat balls, add salt, lemon juice, coriander leaves and grated coconut. Mix gently and serve.

Navane Bisibelebath

Ingredients

  1. Foxtail millet : 1 cup
  2. Red gram : 1/2 cup
  3. Peas : 1/4 cup
  4. Beans : 1/4 cup
  5. Carrot : 1/4 cup
  6. Potatoes : 1/4 cup
  7. Onion : 1
  8. Capsicum : 1
  9. Tomatoes : 2
  10. Tamarind : lemon sized lump
  11. Salt : 1 tsp
  12. Jaggery : 1 tsp
  13. Mustard : 1 tsp
  14. Hing : a pinch
  15. Ghee : 1 tsp
  16. Curry leaves : 2 twigs
  17. Turmeric : 1 tsp
  18. Dry cocnut : 2 tsp

    19. For spice powder
  19. Split bengal gram : 1 tsp
  20. split black gram : 1 tsp
  21. Coriander seeds : 2 tsp
  22. Cloves : 3-4
  23. Cinnamon : 1 piece
  24. Moggu : 3
  25. Red chillies : 4

Recipe

Soak tamarind in water, squeeze and take out the pulp, par boil the vegetables except onion, capsicum and tomatoes. Keep it aside. Roast all the ingredients for spice powder and powder it. Boil Foxtail millet and red gram together with 4 cups of water. Heat ghee in pan,add mustard seeds, Hong, curry leaves, onion, capsicum and fry well. Add tomatoes, boiled vegetables, tamarind pulp, salt, jaggery, turmeric, spice powder and mix well. Roast dry coconut and mix with the above. Add boiled foxtail millet and red gram mixture to this and bring it to a boil. Serve hot with raita or mixture.

Indonesian Salad

Ingredients

  3. Butter : 2 tsp
  4. Garlic flakes : 3-4
  5. Spring Onions (chopped) : 1/4 tsp
  6. Pepper powder : 1/4 tsp
  7. Ginger powder : 1/4 tsp
  8. Salt : 1/4 tsp
  9. Soya sauce : 1/4 tsp
  10. Vinegar : 1/4 tsp

Recipe

Add carrots and cauliflower in boiling water for 5 minutes and drain.Heat butter in a wok and add crushed garlic and fry for 2 minutes Add onions, Soya sauce, vinegar and stir well. Add all the powders and mix well. Cook for few minutes and serve hot.

Crunchy Snack

Ingredients

  1. Flour : 1 cup
  2. Green gram dhal : 2 tsp
  3. Cumin seeds : 1 tsp
  4. Pepper powder : 1/2 tsp
  5. hot oil : 2 tsp
  6. salt : 1/2 tsp
  7. oil for deep frying : 1/2

Recipe

Soak green gram dhal in water for an hour and strain it. Take flour and salt in a bowl.Pour 2 tbsp hot oil and mix well.Add salt, pepper and cumin seeds.Add green gram dhal & mix it. Make a stiff dough using water. Divide the dough Roll it into round thin circles.Cut it into diamond shape. Deep fry in hot oil till crispy.

Vadappi

Ingredients

  1. Rice flour : 1 cup
  2. Cumin seeds : 1 tsp
  3. water : 1/2-3/4 cup
  4. Salt : To taste
  5. Oil(for deep frying) : 250 ml

Recipe

Mix rice flour,salt and cumin seeds. Add enough water and make a soft dough.Divide the dough into 10-12 equal portions.Smear oil on a plastic sheet and flatten each portion into thin puries.Deep fry in hot oil.Serve hot with kaipudi or any chutney.
